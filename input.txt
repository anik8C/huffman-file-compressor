A whisper soft, a quiet light, Chasing away the darkest night.
It starts with thought, a gentle breeze, That sets the busy mind at ease.
It builds in rhythm, heart, and rhyme, A moment stolen out of time.
A breath of words, both swift and free, A quiet, crafted melody.
It brings a smile, it shares a tear, It holds the things we hold most dear.
A simple poem, small and true, Written, for this moment, just for you.
